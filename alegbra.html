Alegbra

Alegbra is all about finding values of certain letters like x + 7 = 13. so first you want to keep x on 1 side without other numbers so you carry 7 behind 13 and because 7 is positive and you have moved it on the other side of the equals symbol so you change the addition sign to subtractionso it becomes x = 13 - 7 which is 6 so x = 6.
