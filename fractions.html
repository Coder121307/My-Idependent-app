Fractions

A fraction is a part of a number, Like 1/3 is a third of 1. the top number is the amount of pieces/parts that are filled known as the numerator, while the bottom number is called the denominator and is the total number of pieces. A mixed fraction is just a normal fraction exept it has a whole number so like 3 1/3 is a mixed fraction so you have 3 wholes and a third of another one
